## Combined version

All raw datasets analysed in this study are publicly available from the original repositories under the accession numbers listed in the Methods. The locked 97-gene program, benchmark and proxy gene sets, processed result tables, figure source data, analysis scripts and reproducibility notes are available at https://github.com/yechengqian1995-source/fjtcm-gastro-bioinfo, release v1.0.4, commit ddf39e7edf0a47c0f4ecd29b95ce514d38d62225, and are archived on Zenodo at 10.5281/zenodo.20458128. Raw single-cell and single-nucleus matrices and large intermediate objects are not redistributed; they can be obtained from the original public repositories.

## Separate data/code version

Data availability: Raw datasets are publicly available from the original repositories under the accession numbers listed in the Methods. Processed result tables, the locked 97-gene program, benchmark gene sets and figure source data are available in the Zenodo archive 10.5281/zenodo.20458128.

Code availability: Analysis and figure-generation scripts are available at https://github.com/yechengqian1995-source/fjtcm-gastro-bioinfo, release v1.0.4, commit ddf39e7edf0a47c0f4ecd29b95ce514d38d62225, and archived at Zenodo 10.5281/zenodo.20458128.
